Social Media Reset Plan (14 Days)

Prototype Article

This reset is designed to reduce compulsive scrolling and restore focus.

Day 1-3: Awareness
- Track total screen time
- Note emotional state before and after scrolling
- Disable non-essential notifications

Day 4-6: Boundaries
- Remove social apps from home screen
- Use app timers (30 to 45 mins/day)
- Keep phone out of bedroom at night

Day 7-9: Replacement Habits
- Replace morning scroll with journaling (5 mins)
- Replace evening scroll with stretching or reading
- Schedule one offline social interaction

Day 10-12: Dopamine Rebalance
- No-scroll first hour after waking
- No-scroll last hour before sleep
- Practice 10 minutes of mindful breathing daily

Day 13-14: Long-Term Plan
- Keep one social app for utility only
- Set weekly digital detox block (half day)
- Review triggers and update your plan monthly

Quick rescue protocol for urge spikes:
1. Pause
2. Name the urge
3. Delay by 10 minutes
4. Do one alternative action

Recovery is not anti-technology. It is pro-intention and pro-wellbeing.
