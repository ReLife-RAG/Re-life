Substance Recovery Roadmap

Prototype Article

Important note:
This is educational prototype content, not emergency medical advice. For urgent risk, contact local emergency services.

Phase 1: Stabilize
- Remove immediate access to substances
- Inform one trusted person
- Build a same-day support list (friend, counselor, hotline)

Phase 2: Protect
- Identify high-risk places and social situations
- Create a transportation and safety backup plan
- Keep hydration, meals, and sleep as daily anchors

Phase 3: Rebuild
- Start simple routines: wake time, movement, check-in
- Attend counseling or support meetings
- Track cravings with time, place, emotion, intensity

Phase 4: Relapse Prevention
- Pre-write a response for high-risk moments
- Use urge surfing and delay strategies
- Practice self-forgiveness after setbacks and return to plan immediately

Daily Recovery Check:
- Did I protect my environment today?
- Did I use one healthy coping tool?
- Did I ask for support when needed?

Healing is a process of repeated, intentional choices.
