Relapse Prevention Strategies

Relapse prevention is about planning before difficult moments happen.

1. Know Your High-Risk Patterns
List people, places, emotions, and times that increase cravings.

2. Create a Fast Response Plan
Pick three healthy alternatives:
- Call a trusted person
- Use urge surfing for 10 minutes
- Take a short walk and hydrate

3. Delay and Decide
When an urge appears, delay action by 20 minutes and review your goals.

4. Track Wins
At the end of each day, write one moment where you chose recovery.

Consistency matters more than perfection.
